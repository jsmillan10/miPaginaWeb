Childhood

I was born in Bucaramanga city and I lived there 11 years with all my family sorrounding me and learning from them all the time.

Adolescence

When I was 12 years, I went to Bogotá with my parents and my brother to continue my studies and learn how is the life in the capital city.

I'm working to explore the feeling of been in another culture outside of my country.

Maybe France or Switzerland

Primary

I make my primary education in Comfenalco school in Bucaramanga. There I start to understand the importance of studying and been an applied person.

High School

I make all my high school at Gimnasio el Hontanar. In this college I start to feel what is to have friends, the importance of working and being respectful with others

Undergraduate

Nowadays, I'm a student at Universidad de los Andes. Here I have understand that everything is possible with effort and willpower. Here I also find out that there are no limits for my goals but the road is neither easy nor impossible.

Sports

I love to play and practice tennis and soccer since I was a child at school. I also enjoy a lot dancing, going to the gym and making exercise in general.

I also enjoy traveling around the world, reading, laughing, hanging out with my friends and family, and smiling as far as I can.

Happiness is the most important thing in my life.

Music

I'm a music lover. I enjoy to listen almost every genre, go to concerts of my favorite artists and playing piano whenever I can.
